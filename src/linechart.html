In 2011, Columbia's endowment sat at $7.7 billion.

Barring a $0.1 billion decrease in the following year, the endowment steadily increased until 2015, when it reached $9.6 billion.

In the following year, however, Columbia's endowment decreased to an even $9 billion.

Since then, Columbia's endowment had once again continued to increase, with the school seeing a 27 percent increase between 2020 and 2021, a $3 billion jump.

This year, however, Columbia's endowment took a big hit, with the school losing over a billion dollars in endowment.

The school reported a 7.6 percent decrease in endowment, a figure much bigger than the losses reported by any other Ivy that has reported their endowment this year.
