Scrollama: Sticky Side Example

In 2011, Columbia's endowment sat at 7.7 billion dollars.

Barring a 0.1 billion dollar decrease in the following year, the endowment steadily increased until 2015, when it reached 9.6 billion dollars.

In the following year, however, Columbia's endowment decreased to an even 9 billion dollars.

Since then, Columbia's endowment had once again continued to increase, with the school seeing a 27% increase between 2020 and 2021, a 3 billion dollar jump.

This year, however, Columbia's endowment took a big hit, with the school losing over a billion dollars in endowment.

The school reported a 7.6% decrease in endowment, a figure much bigger than the losses reported by any other Ivy that has reported their endowment this year.
